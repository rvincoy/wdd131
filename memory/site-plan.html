Memory Game Site Plan

Rolando Vincoy

WDD 131-A3

Overview

Purpose

To create a memory game. The object of the game is to shuffle the cards. There are 8 distinct card faces stored in two cards each. They will be face down and once two cards are opened (opened by clicking) they will be compared and if they do not match, they are closed back down. Game ends when all cards are revealed (matched). The game counts the number of comparisons.

Audience

Public, All ages

Dynamic elements

Dynamically assign the cards by randomizing where they placed on the screen. When a card is clicked, it reveals the hidden card faces overwriting the image of the back of the card. When two cards are compared and they do not match, the cards are flipped back down again.
